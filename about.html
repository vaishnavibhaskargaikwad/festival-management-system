Festival Management System – For Customers/Participants What is it? The Festival Management System is an online platform where customers (festival visitors, participants, or guests) can get all the information and services related to a festival in one place — like event schedules, registration, ticket booking, and more. Features for Customers 1. View Events & Schedules Easily check all upcoming events like music, dance, drama, food stalls, etc. See event date, time, and venue in one place. 2. Register for Events Participate in competitions or cultural programs. Fill out a simple form online — no need to visit in person. 3. Book Tickets (if paid entry) Buy entry passes or event-specific tickets online. Payment options available (optional, based on system). 4. Find Event Locations Map or layout of the festival area. Know exactly where the events or food stalls are. 5. Get Notifications Receive alerts or updates on changes in schedule, new events, etc. 6. Login to View Your Activities See your registrations, downloaded tickets, and participation status. 7. Download Certificates or Passes After attending or participating, customers can download certificates or entry confirmation. 8. Feedback & Ratings Give feedback about the events. Rate your experience with the festival. Benefits for Customers No need to wait in long lines All information is available 24x7 on your mobile or computer Smooth experience from registration to attending Better planning of which events to attend Fast communication with organizers Who can use it? General public attending the festival Students/participants who want to join events Families planning to visit food or fun stalls VIP guests or sponsors (with special access) If you're planning to explain this system to customers, you could say: “This system is like your one-stop online guide for the entire festival. Whether you want to see what’s happening, register, book your ticket, or give feedback — everything is at your fingertips.”
